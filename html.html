Things on my Computer

Context

I like to share what I do on my computer. When it comes to being minimalistic on improving performance in your workplace, home office and overall.

Important things to be Minimalistic

  • A simple beautiful non distracting background
  • just 2 icons, one folder and the bin
  • Hide the taskbar automatically, open it clicking the windows icon on the keyboard.
  • Learn all the commands possible to simplify everything.

Advantages

  1. Reduces your time on distractions
  2. Speeds up your productivity
  3. Enables you to do more stuff
  4. Enhanced focus
  5. Fewer resources and extending the life-span of your hardware

Disadvantages

  1. Memorize where everything is
  2. Having to know all the commands
  3. Transitioning to this setup takes time and many people don't have that
  4. Social Pressure or misunderstanding
  5. Fear of missing out or feelings of deprivation
